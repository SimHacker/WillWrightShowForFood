# Trail — augmentation_and_hypertext 🕯️

> *Augment, don't replace — and connect everything*

The forebears behind the show's **Player-in-the-Middle** tenet: Engelbart's *augmentation*, Nelson's *hypertext*, Weiser's *calm computing*. Several are honored in **memorial mode** — we represent and **discuss** them, and invite the living people who knew them; we never interview or speak as them.
