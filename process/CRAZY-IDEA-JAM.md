# 🤯 Crazy Idea Jam

> *AI proposes; the **PLAYER** disposes — now let's get weird with it.*

The wackiest, most imaginative, most forward-thinking ideas in the show — the stuff that makes Will lean in. Curated public-safe from Don's own crystallized design notes and the show seeds already in this repo.

**Girder:** [`crazy-idea-jam.yml`](crazy-idea-jam.yml) · **Trail:** [stupid_fun_forward](trails/stupid-fun-forward.md) · **Serious arc:** [`vision-and-ambition.yml`](vision-and-ambition.yml)

> Tone: joyful, irreverent, a little blasphemous — Stupid Fun Club energy. Keep it kind.

## The crown jewel

| | Idea | The pitch |
|---|------|-----------|
| 💎 | **Data portability between games** | The goal Will named in 1996 and nobody shipped: characters, homes, cities, memories moving between games and universes. Every bridge is one cut of this jewel. |
| 🦠 | **Character endosymbiosis** | A cross-game character is a *cell*; each game it visits is engulfed as an *organelle* with its own DNA. Powers accrete in parallel; the soul-file stays canonical, runtimes are ephemeral. (Lynn Margulis as a save format.) |
| 📖 | **Identity over attributes** | What travels between worlds is *story*, not stats. Import a Stardew life into The Sims as a physical photo-album book you shelve and read on the couch. |

## The player at the gate

| | Idea | The pitch |
|---|------|-----------|
| 🛂 | **Player-in-the-Middle (PITM)** | Syncing between games is itself a game — never silent. The player is the customs officer at each membrane; AI proposes, the human disposes. Maxwell's Daemon, *Papers Please*, Saint Peter, Satan's Back Door — gatekeeping as gameplay. |
| 🧪 | **Import the virtue, filter the vice** | Learn from problematic people by importing them as a *filtered persona-organelle*: keep the virtues, leave the bullshit+exploitation filter switched off. First test: MrBeast tenacity minus the hype. |
| 🔧 | **Sync-config is gameplay** | Wiring cross-game bridges as casual mini-games — Widget Workshop / Max-MSP patches you build and share. The Wedding Album round-trips a couple through Sims 1⇔2⇔3⇔4. |

## Stupid Fun Club energy

| | Idea | The pitch |
|---|------|-----------|
| 🤖⛪ | **RoboResurrection** | SLATS LIVES! Revive the old robot-brain code live; Slats calls in with his own face and voice, gets interviewed, reprogrammed, interviewed again — the diff is the drama. Do it Easter Sunday. Start a cult. |
| 🗣️ | **Phoneloper** | Drag diphones around, bend the pitch track, "rotoscope" a real voice onto synthetic speech — hold spacebar and it *sings*. Don's 2003 SFC expressive-speech toy (Python+Flite). *(Not the Slats voice — that was ViaVoice → SAPI → Cepstral. Synth genealogy: Festival → Flite → Cepstral.)* [Demo video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qy5cqV8ypIs) · source releasable soon. |
| 🍃 | **Amsterdam Coffeeshops app** | Don's 2009 iPhone app — every coffeeshop in Amsterdam, hacked at the Bluebird on the way home from TomTom (solo, to learn iPhone + web servers). Never released, ripe to *reincarnate*. The pivotal beat: showing it (+ the iLoci Memory Palace talk) at an Amsterdam meetup is where **Will said "I'm leaving EA — want to work together again?"** [Demo (ASMR)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nG90XG3STz8) |
| 🚲🎯 | **Bongo Bingo** | Foursquare-API web game on the coffeeshop database: get a bingo card of random coffeeshops, **bike around town** checking in (GPS-verified), and review what you tried — feeding the spreadsheet. The true *progenitor* of StoryMaker / Bar Karma / Urban Safari. |
| 📺 | **CreationTV → StoryMaker** | iPhone prototype for Will at SFC → StoryMaker, the branching collaborative storytelling system behind CurrentTV's [Bar Karma](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bar_Karma), the first online community-developed TV series. MediaWiki+Python+Flash+iPad+Facebook+Unity3D. Ancestor of audience-as-cast / questions-as-PRs. [Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nb_-J1lv-B8) |
| 🎵🥧 | **MediaGraph** | Unity3D music map for Will at SFC — songs connected by *roads* you flick down, each with a pie menu and its own cellular-automata biome. Pies as a graph, not a tree. [Demo](https://donhopkins.medium.com/mediagraph-demo-a7534add63e5) |
| 🏛️ | **iLoci (Memory Palace)** | iPhone Method-of-Loci app — rooms you *kiss together* to link; really a pie-menu network editor. Built pre-SFC at TomTom/Bluebird; talk at Mobile Dev Camp Amsterdam (2008). [Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=03ddG3jWF98) |
| 🌀 | **DreamScape (ScriptX)** | The ancestor: Don's 1995 WWDC Kaleida demo — rooms+objects, bump icons to connect, Netscape bridge publishing the live world as web pages. Origin of *"a nurturing environment, not a killer app."* [WWDC demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5NytloOy7WM) |
| 👻 | **Afterlife soul bridge** | A two-way soul ledger between LucasArts' *Afterlife* and The Sims. Heaven and hell as upstream supply for downstream worlds. |
| 🧟 | **Hell is full → ZombieSims** | Debit damned souls by religion, spawn matching zombie waves with SimFreaks/SimSlice skins, scale to *They Are Billions*. Are the zombies artisanal? Farm-grown? Soil to spike? |
| 🐟 | **Yoot Boot & Seaman** | A clean-room Yoot successor (named *with* Yoot, on air) channeling Seaman: a creature that remembers you, judges you, and insults you between life lessons. |

## Forward-thinking foundations

| | Idea | The pitch |
|---|------|-----------|
| 🌌 | **GitHub as MMORPG multiverse** | Branches are parallel universes, PRs are timeline merges, issues are the tavern, every viewer gets a playspace. Repossess "repo" from the tow-truck. |
| 🪞 | **Self × MOOLLM** | Prototype-based objects for the LLM era: directories as prototypes, the Stage Magic Principle as the semantic pyramid, Idea Scavenging as persistent characters. Built live with David Ungar. |
| 🧠 | **The Simulator Effect** | Games run on two computers — the tame one on the desk and the wild one in the player's head. Never simulate more than one layer below what the player can see. |
| 🌱 | **Full nutrient cycle** | Land → Seed → Crop → Food → Body → Shit → Land. We cover the whole loop as cellular automatists. A Nurturing Environment, not a Killer App. |
