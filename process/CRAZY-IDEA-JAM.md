# 🤯 Crazy Idea Jam

> *AI proposes; the **PLAYER** disposes — now let's get weird with it.*

Welcome to the reactor. This is where the show keeps the ideas that make Will lean in, raise an
eyebrow, and say *"wait, do that one."* Some are serious enough to bet a decade on. Some are jokes
that turned out to be load-bearing. Most are both. Every one of them is real, sourced from Don's
own crystallized design notes and the show seeds already living in this repo.

Ideas come from **everyone** — not just AI. Humans, the audience, **Will especially**, **Don
even**, famous over-reachers like **Peter Molyneux**, and **you**. Anyone can propose; the player
disposes.

**House rules of the reactor:** keep it kind, keep it true, keep it weird. The wildest idea in the
room still has to *run* one day.

**Girder:** [`crazy-idea-jam.yml`](crazy-idea-jam.yml) · **Trail:** [stupid_fun_forward](trails/stupid-fun-forward.md) · **Serious arc:** [`vision-and-ambition.yml`](vision-and-ambition.yml)

> Tone: joyful, irreverent, a little blasphemous — Stupid Fun Club energy. Keep it kind.

## 💎 The crown jewel

*The goal Will named on a Stanford stage in 1996 — and that **still** hasn't shipped. Everything else here is a facet of this one stone.*

| | Idea | The pitch |
|---|------|-----------|
| 💎 | **Data portability between games** | Characters, homes, cities, and *memories* moving between games and universes. Will said it out loud in 1996; nobody shipped it. Every bridge we build is one cut of this jewel — which is exactly why they're cool. |
| 🦠 | **Character endosymbiosis** | A cross-game character is a *cell*; each game it visits gets engulfed as an *organelle* with its own DNA. Powers accrete in parallel; the soul-file stays canonical, the runtimes are disposable. Lynn Margulis as a save format. Yes, really. |
| 📖 | **Identity over attributes** | What crosses between worlds is *story*, not stats. Import a Stardew life into The Sims as a physical photo-album book you shelve, read on the couch, and leave out for guests. |

## 🛂 The player at the gate

*The constitutional layer: the human stays in the middle of every transformation. The machine never gets the last word.*

| | Idea | The pitch |
|---|------|-----------|
| 🛂 | **Player-in-the-Middle (PITM)** | Syncing between games is *itself a game* — never silent, never automatic. You're the customs officer at every membrane: AI proposes, you dispose. Maxwell's Daemon, *Papers, Please*, Saint Peter at the gate, Satan at the back door — gatekeeping as gameplay. |
| 🧪 | **Import the virtue, filter the vice** | Learn from a problematic genius by importing them as a *filtered persona-organelle*: keep the virtues, leave the "bullshit + exploitation" valve firmly shut. First test subject: MrBeast tenacity, minus the hype. |
| 🔧 | **Sync-config is gameplay** | Turn the boring part — wiring the cross-game bridges — into casual mini-games: Widget Workshop patches you build and swap. The Wedding Album round-trips a couple through Sims 1⇔2⇔3⇔4, each edition a clean organelle. |

## 🤖 Stupid Fun Club energy

*The blasphemous wing. Read with the understanding that every one of these has actually been prototyped, proposed, or sincerely threatened.*

| | Idea | The pitch |
|---|------|-----------|
| 🤖⛪ | **RoboResurrection** | **SLATS LIVES.** Revive the old Stupid Fun Club robot-brain code on air; Slats calls in with his own face and voice, gets interviewed, gets reprogrammed, gets interviewed *again* — and the diff is the drama. Do it Easter Sunday. Start a cult. "It seemed to be the thing to do at the time." |
| 🗣️ | **Phoneloper** | Drag diphones around, bend the pitch track, "rotoscope" a real voice onto synthetic speech — then hold spacebar and it *sings*, an accidental granular-synth instrument. Don's 2003 SFC expressive-speech toy (Python + Flite). *(Not the Slats voice — that was ViaVoice → SAPI → Cepstral. Synth genealogy: Festival → Flite → Cepstral.)* [Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qy5cqV8ypIs) · source releasable soon. |
| 🍃 | **Amsterdam Coffeeshops app** | Don's 2009 iPhone app — every coffeeshop in Amsterdam, hacked at the Bluebird on the way home from his TomTom day job (solo, to learn iPhone + web servers). Never released, ripe to *reincarnate*. The plot twist: showing it (plus the iLoci talk) at an Amsterdam meetup is where **Will said "I'm leaving EA — want to work together again?"** [Demo (ASMR)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nG90XG3STz8) |
| 🚲🎯 | **Bongo Bingo** | A Foursquare-API web game on that coffeeshop database: get a bingo card of random coffeeshops, **bike across town** checking in (GPS-verified), review what you tried — feeding the spreadsheet. The true *progenitor* of StoryMaker / Bar Karma / Urban Safari. |
| 📺 | **CreationTV → StoryMaker** | iPhone prototype for Will at SFC → StoryMaker, the branching collaborative storytelling system behind CurrentTV's [Bar Karma](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bar_Karma) — the first online community-developed TV series, voted and built by its audience, aired on real TV. MediaWiki + Python + Flash + iPad + Facebook + Unity3D. The direct ancestor of audience-as-cast and questions-as-PRs. [Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Nb_-J1lv-B8) |
| 🎵🥧 | **MediaGraph** | Unity3D music map for Will at SFC — arrange songs, connect them with *roads*, flick down the roads to travel. Each song has a pie menu and its own cellular-automata biome that grows and fights its neighbors. Pies as a graph, not a tree. [Demo](https://donhopkins.medium.com/mediagraph-demo-a7534add63e5) |
| 🏛️ | **iLoci (Memory Palace)** | iPhone Method-of-Loci app — rooms you *kiss together* to link and unlink; secretly a pie-menu network editor. Built pre-SFC at TomTom/Bluebird; talk at Mobile Dev Camp Amsterdam (2008). [Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=03ddG3jWF98) |
| 🌀 | **DreamScape (ScriptX)** | The ancestor of it all: Don's 1995 WWDC Kaleida demo — rooms + objects, bump icons together to connect, a Netscape bridge publishing the live world as web pages. Demoed live without crashing. Origin of the mantra *"a nurturing environment, not a killer app."* [WWDC demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5NytloOy7WM) |
| 👻 | **Afterlife soul bridge** | A two-way, round-trippable soul ledger between LucasArts' *Afterlife* and The Sims. Heaven and hell as upstream supply for downstream worlds. The bridge *is* the gameplay. |
| 🧟 | **Hell is full → ZombieSims** | "Hell is full." Debit damned souls by religion, spawn matching zombie waves with SimFreaks / SimSlice skins, scale toward *They Are Billions* horde pressure — then round-trip the carnage back to the hell ledger. Interview the suppliers: are these zombies artisanal? Farm-grown? Soil to spike? |
| 🐟 | **Yoot Boot & Seaman** | A clean-room Yoot successor (named *with* Yoot, on air — working title YOOT BOOT, because BOOT is BOAT in Dutch) channeling Seaman: a creature that remembers you, judges you, and insults you between life lessons. 1999's most forward-thinking idea was treating the player as someone worth being rude to. **He designs it with us; we code it for him** — tower, boat, cruise liner, spaceship, submarine, whatever. And it's time to bring **Seaman back for the LLM era**. *(Yoot Tower itself: delayed/licensing — abstract only, no pressure.)* |
| 🫠 | **Cellular automata as DRM** | The 1991 free-but-not-free demo: play five minutes, and if you haven't bought a license the cellular automata wake up and *melt your city*. The same rules later became the hypnotic "Space Inventory" you can watch for hours. Punishment and beauty from a single engine. |
| 🥧🔥 | **Pie menus put out fires** | UI critic Don Norman complained pie menus made SimCity *too* easy — build fast, oops, nuclear meltdown, merrily on your way. Don's rebuttal: *"Linear menus caused the meltdown. The round menus put the fires out."* A live argument about whether making things easy helps or hurts learning. |

## 🚀 Forward-thinking foundations

*The serious wing. Quieter, but these are the ones that quietly rearrange how everything else works.*

| | Idea | The pitch |
|---|------|-----------|
| 🌌 | **GitHub as MMORPG multiverse** | Branches are parallel universes, PRs are timeline merges, issues are the tavern, every viewer gets a playspace. Repossess "repo" from the tow-truck — Repo Man / Woman / Bot honorifics, quest levels that onboard kids via web-editor PRs, badges via Actions. |
| 🪞 | **Self × MOOLLM** | Prototype-based objects for the LLM era: directories as prototypes, the Stage Magic Principle as the semantic pyramid, Idea Scavenging as persistent characters. Built live with David Ungar; artisanal hand-programming vs. intentional AI. |
| 🧠 | **The Simulator Effect** | Games run on *two* computers — the tame one on the desk and the wild one in the player's head. The whole trick is offloading simulation into the player's brain. Rule of thumb: never simulate more than one layer below what the player can see. |
| 🌱 | **Full nutrient cycle** | Land → Seed → Crop → Food → Body → Shit → Land. We cover the *whole* loop as cellular automatists. Water = donations; fertilizer = tokens. A Nurturing Environment, not a Killer App — the Flower Child nurtures; the buyout extracts. |
| 📊 | **Educational data literacy** | Make the sim *export its guts*. Prof. Upmanu Lall (Columbia) wanted SimCity to spit out spreadsheets so students from any department would learn science through a game they relate to. Modern version: Micropolis & Yoot Tower stream telemetry into d3 / Grafana. Don's ~2002 OPTIMUS proposal — only possible after open-sourcing. |
| 🧩 | **Scriptable simulators** | Push the UI out of the engine and let players reprogram the rest. emscripten + embind let you *subclass the C++ engine in JavaScript* — zones, agents, and robots as plug-ins. Wire Snap!/Blockly on top so kids live-code the sim. PacBot follows the roads eating traffic; the Church of Pacmania worships and feeds it. SimAntics VPL spirit, made easy. |
| 🏙️ | **Towers in Micropolis** | Embed multiple *Yoot Towers* inside your Micropolis city — same Maxis era, shared WASM / embind / SvelteKit stack, so a tower becomes a building in a city. Data portability you can point at. |

## 🌶️ How an idea graduates

An idea earns its way *out* of the jam by becoming runnable — a [show seed](../repo-shows/), a
[schema](../schemas/), a harvested [skill](../skills/) in the cauldron, or a [trail node](cross-links.yml).
Until then, it lives here, simmering, occasionally singing when you hold the spacebar.
