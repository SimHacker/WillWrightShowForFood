📡 **Thomas Cherryhomes** (@tschak / tschak909) — Retrocomputing hacker/engineer; firmware developer and co-founder of **FujiNet**, the open-source software-defined network peripheral that puts the *internet* on retro machines (Atari 8-bit → Apple II, Coleco Adam, MS-DOS RS-232, Atari 2600, MSX, Intellivision, TRS-80 CoCo, and more). "Bus over IP," an `N:` network device, a cross-platform **Game Lobby**, high-score servers, and a disk-less **NOS**. Also runs **IRATA.ONLINE** (a PLATO service) and wrote **PLATOTerm**; evangelist of cross-platform C; writes "The FujiNet Report" in the revived *Compute's Gazette*. Life-message: *"We should not argue to win. Rather, we should argue to give and gain insight."* Describes the project as a "giant tree fort." Proposed Repo Show guest — FujiNet is the archetype of repo-native, community-built, cross-platform retro infrastructure, and a natural real-hardware path for the Apple ][ stack + character bridges. **Paired with Lars Brinkhoff** (PDP-10/ITS) for Retrocomputing Drive tag-team.
