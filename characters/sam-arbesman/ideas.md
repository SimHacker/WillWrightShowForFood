## What Sam has done

**Sam Arbesman** — complexity scientist and author (*The Magic of Code*, *Overcomplicated*,
*The Half-Life of Facts*). Scientist in Residence at Lux Capital. Podcast host who interviewed
**Chaim Gingold** on *Building SimCity* and the history of Maxis — then asked Chaim for an intro
to Don because he is "super into all things Maxis" and curious what a **Maxis 2.0** would look like.

## Shared ground

Chaim's intro (July 2024) connected two people who care about **how complex systems get built
and understood** — Chaim as historian, Sam as complexity scientist, Don as the person still
**building** microworld infrastructure (Micropolis, CAM6, federation). Mail threads touched
**Infernal Research** (Interval, Bell Labs, cellular automata lineage) and Don's congratulations
on *The Magic of Code* (April 2025). A video call was planned but never scheduled — the Repo Show
is the better venue: async repo + live stream.

## The hooks

### 1. Maxis 2.0 — not nostalgia, a design brief
What would Will build today if Maxis were a **platform** again? Sam's complexity lens + Chaim's
history + Don's running code — without competing with Chaim's book show (complementary angles).

### 2. The holy grail: data portability across games
Will's **1996 train-set model**: characters, homes, objects, memories moving between worlds.
**SliceCity** (SimCity inside The Sims) as the shipped precedent. GitHub as durable async state +
Twitch as sync — the two-layer backbone for fan-built federation.

### 3. Complexity science meets city simulation
Sam's books on overcomplicated systems and the half-life of facts ↔ SimCity as **toy
microworld** vs black-box city builder. Glass-box rules, operational runbooks, teaching
*how cities fail*.

### 4. CAM6, Sandspiel, and the cellular-automata family
Don's CAM6 lineage, Dave Ackley's Movable Feast, Scott Draves' generative art — the same
**simple rules → complex behavior** thread Sam writes about. Live CA jam on stream.

### 5. Interval Research and the "what if" generation
Infernal Research thread: Interval, SFC, Gallium — the lab culture that fed Sims, Proxi, and
modern VC deep-tech. Sam's Lux seat as the contemporary echo.

### 6. Building SimCity vs building Micropolis Home now
Chaim documented how EA open-sourced SimCity; Don is building **Micropolis Home** in public.
Honest compare: historian's archive vs engineer's running fork.

### 7. Three-way with Chaim (optional episode)
Book historian + complexity scientist + original toolchain author — Maxis history without
a single narrator dominating.
