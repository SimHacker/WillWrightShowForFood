## Why he's a dream guest

- **He's in the lore.** Drew appeared in **The Sims: House Party** (2000), arriving by limo when a
  party's going great, and a *Drew Carey Show* episode played out inside The Sims. To an audience
  of Sims characters, he's family.
- **He's a master of improv.** Decades hosting the best live improvisation on television. This
  show *is* improvisation — a conversation that invents itself in real time.
- **He's the patron saint of audience participation.** *The Price Is Right* is the cathedral of
  *Come On Down* — the tradition our TicketPR / *Question Is Right* homage grows from.
- **He loves libraries.** A long-time public-library supporter — which rhymes with this show's
  preservation and open-source heart.

We'd invite him simply as a guest with a lot to say — about everything he's done with The Sims,
about the craft of improv, and about how a live audience becomes part of a show. No agenda beyond
a great conversation.
