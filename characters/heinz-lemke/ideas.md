## What Heinz has done

Co-author of **PIXIE** (1969) — radial menus on the PDP-6 with Neil E. Wiseman and John O. Hiles.
PhD on the PIXIE user interface. Decades leading **CARS** and computer assisted medicine / patient-
specific modelling. First-hand witness to Cambridge CAD/HCI dawn and US lab visits in the late 1960s.

## The hooks

### 1. Neil Wiseman — the person behind the citation
Lab culture, leadership, design judgment. Don't let Wiseman collapse into a paper header.

### 2. Cambridge zeitgeist (1967–1970)
PDP-6, light pen, film as documentation, colloquia materials in Don's archive.

### 3. US research-lab trip
Who Heinz met, what systems he saw, what crossed the Atlantic both ways.

### 4. PIXIE → CARS
Graphical man-machine communication for design → model-guided medicine today.

### 5. Preservation
Digitized films, *Flight of the PIXIE*, CHM oral history — honor Wiseman and Hiles through Heinz's voice.
