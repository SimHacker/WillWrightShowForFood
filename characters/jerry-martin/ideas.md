## What Jerry has done

Jerry Martin is a composer, producer, and sound designer best known for the music of The Sims, SimCity 3000, and SimCity 4 — scores that gave those worlds their warm, lived-in feel. At the Maxis division of EA he was Studio Audio Director and Lead Composer. He founded Musicontrol (original music + sound for 200+ national TV/radio commercials — AT&T, Toyota, NBA, Honda), holds a BA from CSU Hayward and an MFA from the Center for Contemporary Music at Mills College, and now releases his own work as Jerry Martin Music and via his BoomBamBoom outlet (recent albums *The House Always Wins* and *Be Tonal*). Don's Maxis colleague on The Sims.
