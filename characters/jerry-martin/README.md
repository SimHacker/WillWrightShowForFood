# Jerry Martin 🎵

*Invitation portrayal. A respectful, source-grounded sketch — not Jerry Martin, and not his words.*

## Who

**Jerry Martin** is a composer best known for writing the music for **The Sims** and **SimCity 3000**
at Maxis. His scores gave those digital worlds their warm, lived-in character — the sound of a place
that feels like home.

## Why a Repo Show

Paired with [David Levitt](../david-levitt/) for a **music + theory** show: how game music is
composed, scored, and made to respond to a living simulation. Also a natural for the audio side of a
Sims team reunion.
