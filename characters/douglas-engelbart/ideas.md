## What Douglas did

**Douglas Engelbart** (1925–2013) — augmenting human intellect; **NLS**; the **mouse** (with Bill
English); hypertext; on-screen windows; real-time collaborative editing; the **1968 Mother of All
Demos**. ACM Turing Award (1997). His 1962 *Augmenting Human Intellect* framework still frames
human–computer symbiosis.

## Living discussants

**Alan Kay**, **Ted Nelson**, **Yoot Saito** (interviewed Doug in Japan, early 1990s), **Don Hopkins**
(PIXIE / light-pen lineage). See [`CHARACTER.yml`](CHARACTER.yml) `memorial.who_can_discuss`.
