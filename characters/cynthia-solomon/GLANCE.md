📼 **Cynthia Solomon** — co-creator of **Logo** (BBN, 1967, with Feurzeig, Papert & Bobrow) and a lifelong pioneer of **computing for children**. Built the MIT Logo culture through the 1970s; researched a children's computer at **Atari Cambridge** in the early 1980s. Author of *Computer Environments for Children* (1986), co-author of the *History of Logo* (HOPL, 2020), Constructionism 2016 & FabLearn 2019 lifetime achievement honoree. On YouTube she keeps a rare **treasure trove** of historical video — Papert, the Minskys, kids at the turtle. A Snap! friend; Don met her at Snap!Con Barcelona.
