## What Arthur has done

Arthur van Hoff — Dutch programmer; great long-time friend of Don with many shared projects. At the Turing Institute (Glasgow) he built GoodNeWS → HyperNeWS → HyperLook (a NeWS/PostScript reimagining of HyperCard) and PdB (an object-oriented C-to-PostScript compiler). On the early Java team at Sun he wrote the Java compiler in Java and worked on the AWT and HotJava browser. Co-founded Marimba (Castanet + Bongo; Danny Goodman wrote the Bongo book).
