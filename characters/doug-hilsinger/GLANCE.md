🐅 **Doug Hilsinger** *(dream guest — outreach sent, no reply yet)* — Bay Area multi-instrumentalist who recorded a complete, track-for-track cover of **Brian Eno's *Taking Tiger Mountain (By Strategy)*** with vocalist **Caroleen Beatty** (released 2004, DBK Works, for the album's 30th anniversary). He played **every instrument** — with a deliberate ban on keyboards, synths, and drum machines — turning Eno's chilly 1974 experiment into something warm and aggressively organic. Made privately for love; they slipped Eno a CD-R at his Long Now lecture, and Eno replied *"I am deeply moved… I like it very, very much."* Don was given a copy in SF and wants to **sing Doug's praises** with Brian, Scott, and Will. The living proof of *honoring a work by transforming it.* See [`taking-tiger-mountain-by-serendipity.md`](taking-tiger-mountain-by-serendipity.md).
