# Scott Kim

Invitation portrayal — **not** Scott Kim. [Standards](../../schemas/portrayal-standards.yml)

**Field:** Puzzle designer & visual artist — ambigrams ("inversions"), puzzle design, symmetry & visual thinking

[Invitation](invitation.md) · [Show seed](../../repo-shows/scott-kim/README.md) · [CARD](CARD.yml)

The master of **inversions** — words lettered so they read the same upside down or mirrored (his 1981 book *Inversions*; co-discoverer of ambigrams with John Langdon, the term coined by his friend **Douglas Hofstadter**). His Stanford PhD under **Donald Knuth**, ***Viewpoint: Toward a Computer for Visual Thinkers***, argued for **"visibility"** — the screen's pixels *are* the program state — a deep cousin of Don's "make the invisible visible," Bret Victor's explorables, and Shneiderman's direct manipulation. He designed the **SGI logo**, was puzzlemaster for **Heaven & Earth** (1992), writes puzzle columns (*Games*, *Discover*, *Scientific American*), and lives in the **Martin Gardner** recreational-math world. **A friend of Will Wright's** — two designers who think in play and possibility.
