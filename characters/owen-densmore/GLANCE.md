🌻 **Owen Densmore** — the **OG "User Interface Flower Child"** (Don's title is an homage to him). At **Sun** he built NeWS's **object-oriented PostScript** — `class.ps`, a Smalltalk-style class system in *pure* PostScript, no interpreter changes ("Object Oriented Programming in NeWS," 1986; the OO-PostScript toolkit paper with David Rosenthal, 1987). Earlier at **Apple** (Mac/Lisa, the Macintosh Printing Architecture); later **Sun Chief Scientist** + the **Java Car**; then **complexity science** (Santa Fe Institute, co-founded the **Redfish Group**, NetLogo community). Core **NeWS gang** (Gosling, Rosenthal, van Hoff) — the living proof of *send code, not commands* (PostScript for code, data, AND graphics). One of the kind elders of the **Silicon Valley Hippie Mafia**.
