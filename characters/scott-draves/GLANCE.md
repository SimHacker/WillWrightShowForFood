🐑 **Scott Draves** ("Spot") — software artist; creator of **Electric Sheep** (distributed, evolving screensaver bred by a genetic algorithm + human votes) and inventor of the open-sourced **Fractal Flame algorithm** (1992), whose images graced a Paul Simon cover and Hawking's *The Grand Design*. His evolving painting **Dreams in High Fidelity** has shown at MoMA, Google, and the Adler Planetarium. Founded **Leaders in Software and Art** with Isabel Walcott Draves; built **Beaker Notebook**; relaunched the Sheep as **Infinidream** (2026). Studied under **two Andys** — **van Dam** at Brown (his thesis *fnord*, co-written with Nick Thompson, ran Banchoff's 4D art) and **Witkin** at CMU; long engineering career (Transmeta, DreamWorks, Google, Two Sigma, CTRL-labs/Meta, Stability AI). Don's old friend — they met in the **Bay Area in the early '90s** via Don's housemate **Nick Thompson** (Spot's Brown collaborator, then at SGI, who brought Scott home), reconnected at **CMU**, and Don introduced him to **Brian Eno** after the 2006 Long Now talk with Will Wright. Recently collaborated with Don on the Electric Sheep Unity client.
