## `6heads/` — the ELAVIL anatomical heads

Photos (found online) of the same model that gave **Don's 1990 Mountain View household its name —
"6Heads."** It's an **ELAVIL (amitriptyline HCl)** anatomical human head: a **1974 MSD / Merck**
pharmaceutical gift to doctors, with a removable skull panel and a lift-out plastic brain (came with a
"the human brain" booklet). **Don's house had six of them, arranged on the mantelpiece.**

Used to illustrate the 6Heads backstory — how Don met Scott (via housemate Nick Thompson) — in
[`../ideas.md`](../ideas.md#11-the-6heads-house--1990-mountain-view-banter--storytelling) and
[`../correspondence.yml`](../correspondence.yml).
