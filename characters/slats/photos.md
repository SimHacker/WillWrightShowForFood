# Slats — photos

*Primary-source photos of the real **Stupid Fun Club** robot whose brain Don Hopkins programmed and
whom we portray as the fictional **Slats**. The robot improvised hidden-camera scenes with real
people around Berkeley (see the [One Minute Movies](one-minute-movies.md)).*

## The robot

![Slats's face — horizontal metal slats with two rectangular eyes and a mouth slot (the source of the name)](slats-face.png)

The face is the namesake: a stack of horizontal metal **slats** with cut-out eyes and mouth.

![Slats indoors at the Stupid Fun Club shop in Emeryville, beside a window, with the cast Stupid Fun Club emblem](slats-with-sfc-emblem.png)

Slats at the **Stupid Fun Club** workshop in **Emeryville**, wearing the cast SFC globe emblem.

## On the street (Berkeley — Au Coquelet)

The street shoots centered on **Au Coquelet Cafe Restaurant**, 2000 University Ave, Berkeley — one of
Don's favorite, most-frequent hangouts (now closed). [Yelp](https://www.yelp.com/biz/au-coquelet-cafe-restaurant-berkeley)

![Don holding a coffee and muffin in front of Au Coquelet Cafe Restaurant, Slats beside him at dusk](slats-berkeley-street-dusk.png)

Don with a coffee and muffin in front of **Au Coquelet**, Slats at his side, at dusk.
