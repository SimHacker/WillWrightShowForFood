🗄️ **David S. H. Rosenthal (DSHR)** — Co-developer of **NeWS** with James Gosling at Sun (co-author of *The NeWS Book*), and — uniquely — also a key architect of its winning rival **X11** (he wrote the **ICCCM** and holds an X security patent). Worked on **Andrew** at CMU with Gosling; **employee #4 and chief scientist at Nvidia**; then **Chief Scientist of LOCKSS** (Lots Of Copies Keep Stuff Safe) at Stanford Libraries — long-term **digital preservation** (2025 Paul Evan Peters Award). Born 1948, Cambridge UK.

Warm, mutually-respectful long-term friend of Don since the X10/X11/NeWS window-system wars. His 2024 "X at 40" / "Why X beat NeWS" posts (and his "History of Window Systems," "Pie Menus," and "Recreational Bugs" pieces on blog.dshr.org) repeatedly surface and amplify Don's NeWS/pie-menu history.

**Co-guest on Heinz Lemke's PIXIE show** — same Cambridge **PDP-7/340 + Titan** ~1970; trio with **Lars Brinkhoff** (emulation). [`../heinz-lemke/invitation.md`](../heinz-lemke/invitation.md)
