## What David has done

David S. H. Rosenthal (DSHR) — Co-developer of **NeWS** with James Gosling at Sun (co-author of
*The NeWS Book*), and — uniquely — also a key architect of its winning rival **X11** (he wrote
the **ICCCM** and holds an X security patent). Worked on **Andrew** at CMU with Gosling;
employee #4 and chief scientist at Nvidia; then Chief Scientist of **LOCKSS** (Lots Of Copies Keep
Stuff Safe) at Stanford Libraries — long-term digital preservation (2025 Paul Evan Peters Award).
Born 1948, Cambridge UK.
