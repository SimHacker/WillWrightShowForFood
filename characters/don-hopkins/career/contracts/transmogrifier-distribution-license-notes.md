# Transmogrifier — EA/Maxis distribution license (notes)

Two distinct legal layers:

1. **EA Tools End User License** — click-through shown to downloaders. ✅ In repo: [`transmogrifier-ea-tools-eula.txt`](transmogrifier-ea-tools-eula.txt) (Wayback Dec 2004, harvested from Don's `big-prompts.txt` IA cache).
2. **Don's distributor agreement** — Maxis grant to Don Hopkins to host the installer on donhopkins.com only. **Not yet in repo** — summary below from renovation plan + Don memory.

Summarized from Don's [Transmogrifier Renovation Plan](https://github.com/SimHacker/WillWrightShowForFood/blob/main/repo-shows/will-wright/sources/2004-02-05-transmogrifier-renovation-plan/article.md) (2004) and Drupal node-17 harvest.

Wayback index: `DonHopkins/projects/willwrightshowforfood/research/sources/transmogrifier-wayback-INDEX.yml`

---

## Model

**Not open source.** EA/Maxis granted Don Hopkins permission to distribute **The Sims Transmogrifier** Windows binary to end users under a **click-through license** — user must read and agree on donhopkins.com before download.

Don was the **authorized distributor**, not the copyright owner of The Sims assets Transmogrifier manipulates.

---

## Known terms (original distributor agreement — Don summary)

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Distribution channel** | Only from Don's web site (`donhopkins.com` / thesimstransmogrifier.com) |
| **User agreement** | License displayed; user must agree before download |
| **CD / physical** | Don not permitted to sell/distribute on CD without separate permission |
| **Third-party redistribution** | Third parties **may not** redistribute Transmogrifier in any form |
| **Reality on the ground** | Unofficial mirrors proliferated without click-through; Don turned down many re-host requests |

---

## End-user license (EA Tools EULA) — key points

Full text: [`transmogrifier-ea-tools-eula.txt`](transmogrifier-ea-tools-eula.txt)

| Clause | Summary |
|--------|---------|
| Grant | Non-transferable, non-exclusive; personal **noncommercial** use with EA products |
| Fan content | May post created materials on personal noncommercial site with required EA disclaimer |
| Restrictions | No reverse engineering, redistribution, commercial use, server emulators |
| Support | None — do not contact EA customer support |
| Governing law | US copyright + California law; © 2000 Electronic Arts Inc. |

## Rumored update (never received in writing)

Don read online that Maxis changed policy to allow **pay sites** to offer transmogrified objects to subscribers. Don was **never notified** of updated license text; original agreement still displayed on site as of 2004 renovation plan.

---

## Don's proposed improvement (2004)

Installer that **requires** agreement before install/run — so even unofficial mirror downloads would still hit the license. Would enable Maxis to widen **installer** redistribution while preserving agreement flow.
