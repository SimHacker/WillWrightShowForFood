## Yoot Saito interviews Alan Kay (1993)

**MACWORLD Japan, 1993** — Yoot interviewing Alan Kay on personal computing, Dynabook, Smalltalk,
Xerox PARC, education, and simulation. Companion to Yoot's **Douglas Engelbart** interview marking
the 25th anniversary of the 1968 demo (Engelbart transcript still sought; Alan companion is published).
