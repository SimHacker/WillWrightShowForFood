# Reflecting on Self — Narcissa's Mirror (dream guest: David Ungar)

**Dream Repo Show episode** with **David Ungar** — co-creator of **Self** (prototype objects, adaptive JIT, generation-scavenging GC).

David was **very open and influential** — generous in person, on **self-interest!**, at the Kaleida **Conscientious Objectors** meetup, and across decades of public work. Don's view: **huge credit** for where interactive computing landed, even though Self doesn't run on everybody's phone. **In spirit it does** — JavaScript's object model, HotSpot's adaptive JIT lineage, MOOLLM's directories-as-prototypes.

**Working title:** *Reflecting on Self: Narcissa's Mirror* — Self **mirrors** + narcissus wordplay + MOOLLM's `# import self from self`. Confirm title spelling with David on air.
