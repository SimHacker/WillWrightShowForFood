## Who

**Jamie Doornbos** was a lead programmer on **The Sims** at Maxis and one of the principal architects
of the game's Sim behavior and AI. He worked at the heart of the simulation — the **SimAntics**
behavior engine, smart objects advertising their services, and the motive/needs system that made
autonomous Sims feel like they had inner lives. He's discussed the engineering and design of the
Sims' simulation publicly.

## Why a Repo Show

Jamie was Don's colleague on The Sims, and few people can explain *how the magic actually worked* —
how simple rules and advertised affordances produced emergent life — as directly as one of its lead
engineers. A natural voice for the [Sims team reunion](../../repo-shows/REPO-SHOWS.yml).
