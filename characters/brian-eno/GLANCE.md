🎛️ **Brian Eno** *(dream guest)* — musician, composer, producer, and visual artist; pioneer of **ambient music** (*Music for Airports*) and a lifelong champion of **generative music and art** (Bloom, *77 Million Paintings*). Producer of Bowie, Talking Heads, U2, Roxy Music, Devo; co-author of *Oblique Strategies*; he coined **"The Long Now"** and helped found the Long Now Foundation, where in 2006 he and **Will Wright** gave *Playing with Time*. Don met Brian after that talk and introduced him to **Scott Draves** (Electric Sheep) at Scott's SF apartment. A dream fourth voice in a generative-systems conversation — reachable via **Will** and **Scott**.
