## What Eric has done

Eric Hedman ("Irk") — animator, artist, and game designer. A character animator on the original The Sims (hand-tuned walk cycles, idles, and gestures for the vitaboy pipeline; the legendary canWiggle incident — Don checked in the debug; they're still great buddies), he went on to design Sims expansion packs (House Party, Livin' Large, Hot Date, Unleashed, and more) — building objects like the mechanical bull ('my baby'), the love bed, and the hot tub, and helping develop the cross-discipline object-prototyping pipeline (simplified 3D-Studio-Max objects rendered as stand-ins, then handed off and refined across artist → animator → programmer, iterated daily in the sandbox build). He animated vignettes and a laser blaster for the Drew Carey Show tie-in. Later he worked at Disney, Jim Henson, Nickelodeon, and others. Today he's an independent creator making adventures, visual novels, games, and VR/AR/MR — including Aether HeXXen (a 'space witch' visual novel) — mostly hand-crafted 2D/3D art and animation. His YouTube channel @irkinteraction is full of warm, funny, detailed Sims-development stories.
