## Why this is here

When Terry (or anyone) opens `characters/terry-winograd/`, the pleasant surprise should be:
*your seminar's whole catalog*, not a stub biography. CS547 **People, Computers, and Design**
was the open, recorded, one-guest-a-week public seminar — the direct forebear of the Repo Show.
