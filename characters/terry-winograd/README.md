## A forebear of the whole quest

Terry's open, public **CS547 "People, Computers, and Design"** seminar at Stanford is the direct
ancestor of the Repo Show — one guest a week, open to everyone, recorded for posterity. When Don
and Terry were colleagues at **Interval Research**, Terry invited Don to the seminar where
**Will Wright** gave his 1996 *"Interfacing to Microworlds"* talk — the Dollhouse preview that sent
Don to Maxis to work on The Sims. Decades later, that talk survived only in **Terry's CS547 video
archive** at Stanford Library; Don emailed Terry at the start of COVID, the archivists scanned the
DVD once the library reopened, and the talk came back to light (now mirrored, transcribed, and
transcluded in [the 1996 source bundle](../../repo-shows/will-wright/sources/1996-04-26-winograd-interfacing-to-microworlds/)).
Terry has been quietly central to this whole quest, across decades — with deep gratitude.
