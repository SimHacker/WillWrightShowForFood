## A forebear of the whole quest

Terry's open, public **CS547 "People, Computers, and Design"** seminar at Stanford is the direct
ancestor of the Repo Show — one guest a week, open to everyone, recorded for posterity. When Don
and Terry were colleagues at **Interval Research**, Terry invited Don to the seminar where
**Will Wright** gave his 1996 *"Interfacing to Microworlds"* talk — the Dollhouse preview that sent
Don to Maxis to work on The Sims.
