## What Terry has done

**Terry Winograd** — Stanford HCI professor; host of **CS547 "People, Computers, and Design"**
(1990s): one luminary per week, open to the public, recorded for posterity, real Q&A. The direct
forebear of the Repo Show format. [`media/cs547-ARCHIVE.md`](media/cs547-ARCHIVE.md) catalogs
339 talks.

## Shared ground

Don has worked with Terry on **preserving and surfacing CS547 recordings**. Verified facts:
- **Will Wright**, April 26, 1996 — *Interfacing to Microworlds* (Dollhouse → The Sims preview).
  Don uploaded to YouTube, transcribed, wrote the Medium retrospective.
- **James Gosling**, December 1, 1995 — *Bringing Behavior to the Internet* (Java). Don found the
  tape, uploaded, transcribed; appears on camera (~1:00:30) asking security questions.

Constructionist threads (Micropolis, visual programming) connect Terry's seminar tradition to
today's Repo Show. See [`correspondence.yml`](correspondence.yml).

## The hooks

### 1. CS547 as forebear — format genealogy on air
One guest, open seminar, durable recording, honest Q&A. How Terry ran it; how the Repo Show
inherits and updates it (GitHub stage + live stream).

### 2. Screen the 1996 Will Wright talk — full circle with Will
*Interfacing to Microworlds* as the flagship anchor. Terry hosted; Don preserved; Will returns
to decode his own 1996 words live.

### 3. Gosling 1995 — Java, NeWS, and Don on camera
The talk that bridges **NeWS** culture to the web. Don's first-hand questions about security and
behavior on the network — still relevant.

### 4. Catalog the treasure trove — pick the next restoration
[`media/cs547-catalog.tsv`](media/cs547-catalog.tsv) as a live "what should we digitize next?"
segment with Terry's curatorial eye.

### 5. SHRDLU, language, and microworlds
Terry's own AI/HCI lineage (blocks world) ↔ Will's microworld philosophy ↔ modern LLM agents —
without hype: **what microworlds teach that chatbots don't**.

### 6. Constructionist education — Micropolis Class
Open tools, explorable rules, credited ideas in public repos. Terry as elder host blessing the
format — or correcting it.
