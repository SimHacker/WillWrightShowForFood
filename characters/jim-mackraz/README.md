## Who

**Jim Mackraz** led the **Core Technology Group (CTG)** on **The Sims** at Maxis — the team
leadership that helped turn Will Wright's wildly open-ended vision into a product that actually
shipped. Don credits him with keeping the team (and Don himself) focused enough to finish.

## Why a Repo Show

A natural voice for the [Sims team reunion](../../repo-shows/REPO-SHOWS.yml), especially on
leadership and how an ambitious, chaotic project came together and shipped.
