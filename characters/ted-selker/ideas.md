## What Ted has done

Ted Selker — HCI researcher and inventor best known for the TrackPoint pointing stick (the red nub on IBM ThinkPad keyboards). He led IBM Almaden's User Systems Ergonomics Research group and its New Paradigms for Using Computers (NPUC) workshops, taught at the MIT Media Lab (Context-Aware Computing; 'considerate computing'), and co-directed the Caltech/MIT Voting Technology Project.
