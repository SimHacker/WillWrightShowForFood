☕ **James Gosling** — Father of Java; original designer/implementer of the **Andrew** window system (CMU, with David Rosenthal) and **NeWS/SunDew** (Sun), both network window systems that predated X11. Also wrote **Gosling Emacs** (sold by UniPress; RMS's "Evil Software Hoarder Emacs"). Self-described record-holder for "the largest number of cheesy little extension languages."

Old friend and colleague of Don: Don worked on Gosling's Emacs at UniPress and on **NeWS** at Sun (Gosling's 1990 email convinced Don to join), under **Warren Teitelman** (NeWS Toolkit manager). At **Alvey 1985** Gosling and Rosenthal published **SunDew** + **Andrew** in the same workshop as Teitelman's retrospective — [`window-systems-lineage.yml`](window-systems-lineage.yml). Don attended Gosling's **Dec 1, 1995** Stanford CS547 talk (youtube.com/watch?v=dgrNeyuwA8k).

The big idea worth making runnable again: NeWS **sent a program, not a data structure** — PostScript for code, graphics, AND data ("the Axis of Eval"), AJAX-before-AJAX but coherent. Proposed Repo Show guest, ideally in a **NeWS reunion** group show with David Rosenthal, Arthur van Hoff, Owen Densmore, and Duncan Mak (NeWS-in-the-browser).
