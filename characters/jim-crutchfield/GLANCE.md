🦋 **James P. Crutchfield** ("Chaos") — physicist of complexity. UC Davis Distinguished Professor + director of the **Complexity Sciences Center**; SFI external faculty; Art & Science Laboratory. One of the UC Santa Cruz **Dynamical Systems Collective** (with Farmer, Packard, Shaw) — "Geometry from a Time Series" (1980) and the *Eudaemonic Pie* roulette computers. Pioneered **video feedback** as a dynamical system, the **"edge of chaos"** + **evolving cellular automata to compute** (with Melanie Mitchell), and founded **computational mechanics** (ε-machines). The "in" for a Repo Show: **video feedback + cellular automata** — recurse, digress, iterate — alongside Don's CAM6 and Scott Draves's generative art.
