(formerly BYOB, "Build Your Own Blocks"), the block-based visual language used in UC Berkeley's *Beauty and Joy of Computing* curriculum, with design input + documentation by Brian Harvey. Built it on his own Morphic.js framework; Snap! has first-class lists, procedures, AND continuations (enough for the Y combinator).
