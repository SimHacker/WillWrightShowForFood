🔲 **Norman Margolus** — physicist & computer scientist (MIT CSAIL; PhD under Edward Fredkin). With **Tommaso Toffoli** he built the **CAM-6** cellular-automata machine and co-wrote ***Cellular Automata Machines*** (MIT Press, 1987) — the very book/hardware **Don's CAM6 emulator** follows. Invented the **block cellular automaton** + **Margolus neighborhood**; built reversible, universal CA (billiard-ball computers, the Critters rule); proved the **Margolus–Levitin theorem** (a physical speed limit on computation, with Lev Levitin); themes of **computronium** / **programmable matter** / **reversible computing**. A good friend of **Henry Minsky** — Don met him in **Boston** and talked through all of this. Natural CA summit with Don's CAM6, Scott Draves, and Jim Crutchfield.
