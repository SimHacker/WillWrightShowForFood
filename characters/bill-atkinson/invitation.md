# In memoriam — Bill Atkinson (1951–2025)

*Not an invitation — a tribute. Legacy K-line, honored with sourced material only.*
[Portrayal standards](../../schemas/portrayal-standards.yml)

Bill Atkinson gave the Macintosh its graphical soul — QuickDraw, MacPaint, and **HyperCard**,
the hypermedia "stacks of cards" that let non-programmers build and link software, years before
the web. That idea carried directly into this repo's lineage: Arthur van Hoff built **HyperLook**
(GoodNeWS → HyperNeWS), a NeWS/PostScript reimagining of HyperCard, and **SimCity ran inside it**.

We celebrate his documented public work; we do not speak as him.

— Don Hopkins, Micropolis Class
