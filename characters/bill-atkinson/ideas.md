## What Bill has done

Bill Atkinson (1951–2025) — Apple employee #51 and architect of the Mac's graphical soul: QuickDraw, MacPaint (FatBits, the selection lasso, marching ants), the menu bar, Atkinson dithering, and HyperCard — the hypermedia stack-of-cards that put programming in non-programmers' hands and prefigured the web. EFF Pioneer Award (1994); later a renowned nature photographer.
