## Why it matters

- **Simlish origins on tape.** Will's mandate was emotive gibberish with no fixed meaning; Kearin
  proposed the improv game *Foreign Poet* and brought Lawlor as his female counterpart. This
  recording is them **doing exactly that in English** while the game runs — thought bubbles,
  personality meters, disasters, and bugs as prompts.
- **Not SimAntics programming** — voice/session artifact, not Edith. Thematic link: objects advertise
  behavior; **voice carries feeling** while icons/thoughts carry legible meaning.
- **Show segment:** pair with [`schemas/language-simlish.yml`](../../../../schemas/language-simlish.yml),
  Claire Curtin / Jerry Martin guest arcs, [`sims-play-along-narration`](../../../../repo-shows/will-wright/sims-play-along-narration.yml),
  and the [**Performance Space**](../../../../process/performance-space/README.md) design doc.

## Ethics

Gerri Lawlor died **January 2019**. Discuss and cite with memorial respect; **do not voice her as a
character** on air. See [`RULES-AND-ETHICS.md`](../../../../process/RULES-AND-ETHICS.md).
