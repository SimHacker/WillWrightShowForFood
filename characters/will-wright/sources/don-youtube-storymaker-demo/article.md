# Stupid Fun Club StoryMaker demo

*YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_2yEHs_WLzQ · "Stupid Fun Club StoryMaker Demo" (yt-dlp)*

## What it was

**StoryMaker** — collaborative branching storytelling stack for Will's **CurrentTV / Bar Karma** era:

- MediaWiki front + Python back  
- Flash client in wiki pages  
- Geolocated scenes, Google Maps/KML  
- iPad MapKit app, Facebook album import/export  
- Voice-synth story reading  
- Unity3D branching-story visualizer (**MediaGraph** lineage)

**Progenitor:** Don's Amsterdam **Coffeeshops** iPhone app + **BONGO BINGO** (Foursquare bingo on coffeeshop spreadsheet) — Will saw it at a meetup and re-hired Don from TomTom.
