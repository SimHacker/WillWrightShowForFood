# Flight of the PIXIE — Yuja Wang

Don's **homage edit** — not the 1969 PIXIE paper or original film.

**Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jDrqR9XssJI

Clips from the historic 1969 PIXIE demonstration film (Cambridge Univ Library archives,
digitized by **David Chapman**, credited on YouTube), cut in **Adobe After Effects** to Yuja Wang's
*Flight of the Bumblebee*. Visual story: pie-menu interaction as rapid bumblebee flight.

Homage to **Neil E. Wiseman**, **Heinz U. Lemke**, and **John O. Hiles** — authors of
*PIXIE: A New Approach to Graphical Man-Machine Communication* (1969 CAD Conference, Southampton).

Sent in [2020-promised-links](../2020-promised-links-maxscript-pipeline/article.md) alongside Sims pipeline demos and the [Aether](../don-youtube-N1Y-29tX0Co-aether/README.md) plug-in.
