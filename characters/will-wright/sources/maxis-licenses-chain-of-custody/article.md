### What each contract establishes

**1 — Work for hire.** Don's Unix/HyperNeWS SimCity port: all IP to DUX and Maxis; royalty formula (5% / 2.5%) documented.

**2 — Ten-year term.** Exclusive Unix/X license to DUX; **§7(a): Licensor retains all rights in New or Translated Versions developed by Publisher** — the clause Chuck Normann needed in 2006. Term **10 years** from Jul 1991 → expired Jul 2001. Exhibit A: Mac SimCity shipping in US; target platforms Unix/X (MS-DOS/Windows explicitly excluded).

**3 — GPL with Exhibit A.** EA permits GPL v3 release for OLPC with trademark/QA stipulations (airplane disaster removed, multiplayer disabled, EA splash, SimCity name QA gate → **Micropolis** fork).
