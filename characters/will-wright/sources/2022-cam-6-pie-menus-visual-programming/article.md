## Scott Kim *Viewpoint* (1988)

Don's defense of visual-first programming — Scott's PhD demo (Knuth advisor, Xerox PARC Cedar/Dorado):

- https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9G0r7jL3xl8
- Thesis: [Viewpoint dissertation PDF](http://www.scottkim.com.previewc40.carrierzone.com/viewpoint/viewpoint-dissertation.pdf)

Thesis claim: *"Only by treating the screen itself as a first class citizen will we be able to build computers that are truly for visual thinkers."*

Don argues **Excel** is a visual programming language (2D grid, continuous recalc) — extending Brad Myers' 1989 critique with 31 years of follow-on work (Garnet, Amulet, programming by demonstration).

Chaim's survey: [Gadget Background Survey (HARC, 2017)](http://chaim.io/download/Gingold%20(2017)%20Gadget%20(1)%20Survey.pdf)
