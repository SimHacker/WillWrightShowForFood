## What Becky has

Rebecca **Heineman** (Burger Becky, Interplay co-founder) ported SimCity to the **Apple IIgs** during the Interplay SimCity CD / enhanced era. Market collapsed before ship; she archived everything.

| Asset | Status |
|-------|--------|
| IIgs port source | Becky owns modifications; willing to release |
| DOS base source | In archive |
| Art + sound | Complete |
| Music | Needed IIgs redo — only missing piece |
| Shipping binary | Released to community archives |

CallApple write-up: https://www.callapple.org/software/the-long-fabled-simcity-for-the-apple-iigs-exists/

Becky (Jul 2019): sole programmer on the IIgs version.
