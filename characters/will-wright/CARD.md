## ⚡ Abilities / Superpowers

- **⚡ Simulator Effect** — Players imagine *far more* than is actually simulated.
  Conjures rich mental models from sparse cues. *(He coined the term.)*
- **🧰 Toys, Not Games** — Makes systems open-ended sandboxes instead of win/lose
  contests. Passive: everything becomes a possibility space.
- **🧠 Distributed Object AI** — "The objects are all kind of advertising." Behavior
  lives *in the objects*, not the character — enabling emergence and modding.
- **🏗️ Hobby Model** — Turns a product into an extensible hobby ("like a train set")
  powered by user-created, shareable content. Counters the "movie model."
- **🎨 Implication > Simulation** — "Implication is more efficient than simulation."
  Fools the player into perceiving more depth than the code contains. Keeps the
  numbers out of the player's face; shows systems *graphically*.
- **🌱 Procedural Amplification** *(Spore)* — A small input blooms into vast content
  (creative amplification); shifts the player from character to *world-builder*.
- **🌈 Emergent Identity** *(The Sims)* — Relationships modeled as emergent and
  player-defined rather than fixed, opening a safe space to explore and tell stories.
