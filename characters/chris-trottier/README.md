## Who

**Chris Trottier** was **Lead Designer at Maxis (1997–2008)** and a key designer on **The Sims** and
later Sims titles — a central voice in the game's content and systems design. Don associates him with
**"design by accretion"**: the patient practice of building a design up layer by layer, detail by
detail, until the whole thing comes alive.

## Why a Repo Show

There's already a flagship seed for him — [design by accretion](../../repo-shows/REPO-SHOWS.yml) —
about how a Sims-scale design grows in dialogue with the "cauldron." He's also a natural voice for
the Sims team reunion.
