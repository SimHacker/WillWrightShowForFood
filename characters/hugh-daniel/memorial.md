# In memoriam — Hugh Daniel (~2013)

*Memorial mode: we represent and discuss his work and the stories friends tell; we do not interview him or speak as him.*
[Memorial standards](../../schemas/portrayal-standards.yml#memorial_mode)

Hugh Daniel was the connective tissue of a whole scene. He worked closely with **Ted Nelson on
Project Xanadu**, hacked **NeWS** with **Don** in the Grasshopper Group (with John Gilmore), and
managed Gilmore's **FreeS/WAN** Linux IPsec project. Don first saw him at MIT's 545 Tech Square
wearing a winged Xanadu cap during a demo; his last birthday featured a fluorescent rice-crispy
cake glowing with LEDs. After he passed, John Gilmore carried his ashes to **Amsterdam**, scattered
along a Jordaan canal — with more planned for space.

We remember him through the people who loved him — **Ted Nelson** and **Don** — and the
Xanadu / hypertext / NeWS lineage he helped weave.

We celebrate his documented public work; we do not speak as him.

— Don Hopkins, Micropolis Class
