# In memoriam — Hugh Daniel (1962–2013)

*Memorial mode: we represent and discuss his work and the stories friends tell; we do not interview him or speak as him.*
[Memorial standards](../../schemas/portrayal-standards.yml#memorial_mode)

Hugh Daniel was the connective tissue of a whole scene. He was an early **sysop of The WELL**
(c. 1985) — one of the people who proved an online community could have real heart — worked closely
with **Ted Nelson on Project Xanadu**, and helped distribute **GoodNeWS**, the NeWS-based
Macintosh-look-alike window manager (with HyperCard- and MacDraw-like tools) that grew into
HyperNeWS and then Arthur van Hoff's **HyperLook** — the very environment SimCity later ran inside.
He hacked **NeWS** with **Don** in the Grasshopper Group (with John Gilmore) and managed Gilmore's
**FreeS/WAN** Linux IPsec project. Don first saw him at MIT's 545 Tech Square wearing a winged
Xanadu cap during a demo; his last birthday featured a fluorescent rice-crispy cake glowing with
LEDs. After he passed, John Gilmore carried his ashes to **Amsterdam**, scattered along a Jordaan
canal — with more planned for space.

We remember him through the people who loved him — **Ted Nelson** and **Don** — and the
Xanadu / hypertext / NeWS lineage he helped weave.

We celebrate his documented public work; we do not speak as him.

*This is a collaborative memorial, not a signed work. Those who knew Hugh — family, friends, and colleagues — are warmly invited to edit, correct, and add their own memories, documents, and stories whenever they're ready.*
