## What Hugh has done

Hugh Daniel (1962–2013) — hacker, evangelist, and irreplaceable social connector. He was an early sysop of The WELL (c. 1985), worked closely with Ted Nelson on Project Xanadu, helped distribute GoodNeWS (the NeWS-based Macintosh-look-alike that became HyperNeWS → van Hoff's HyperLook), hacked NeWS with Don Hopkins in the Grasshopper Group (with John Gilmore), and managed John Gilmore's FreeS/WAN Linux IPsec project. First spotted at MIT's 545 Tech Square in a winged Xanadu cap; remembered with a glowing LED rice-crispy cake and ashes scattered along an Amsterdam canal.
