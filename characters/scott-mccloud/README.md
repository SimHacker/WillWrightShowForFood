## Who

**Scott McCloud** is a cartoonist and comics theorist best known for **_Understanding Comics: The
Invisible Art_** (1993) — itself a comic — followed by *Reinventing Comics* (2000), *Making Comics*
(2006), and the graphic novel *The Sculptor* (2015). His ideas reshaped how creators think about the
reader's role: **closure** (the imagination filling the gap between panels), **masking** (abstract
characters living in detailed worlds), and **amplification through simplification**.

## Why a Repo Show

*Understanding Comics* was a **design bible** for The Sims. Masking — photorealistic environments
with abstract, iconic Sims — let players project themselves and their stories into the game, and the
gaps invited the imagination to do the rest. Don credits McCloud directly in the Sims Transmogrifier
documentation. The same principle runs through Micropolis: the sparse model on screen, and the rich
model the player builds in their head.
