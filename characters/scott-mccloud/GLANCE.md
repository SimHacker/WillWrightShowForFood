💭 **Scott McCloud** — cartoonist & comics theorist, author of **_Understanding Comics_** (1993), *Reinventing Comics*, *Making Comics*, and *The Sculptor*. His ideas — **closure**, **masking**, **amplification through simplification** — were a design bible for The Sims (abstract characters in detailed worlds; the player's imagination filling the gaps). A patron saint of Don's design thinking.
