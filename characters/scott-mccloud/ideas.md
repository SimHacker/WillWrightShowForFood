## What Scott has done

Scott McCloud is a cartoonist and comics theorist best known for _Understanding Comics: The Invisible Art_ (1993), followed by _Reinventing Comics_ (2000) and _Making Comics_ (2006), and the graphic novel _The Sculptor_ (2015). His ideas — closure (the imagination filling the gap between panels), masking (abstract characters in detailed worlds), and amplification through simplification — reshaped how creators think about reader participation and identification.
