## What Vanessa has done

Vanessa Freudenberg (d. 2025) — creator of SqueakJS, a JavaScript implementation of the Squeak Smalltalk virtual machine that brought Smalltalk live into the web browser. Longtime contributor to Squeak/Etoys, the playful Plopp/Plop projects, and the Croquet / Multisynq synchronized object work. After SqueakJS she had sharp, insightful things to say about WebAssembly — the live-VM-in-the-browser question Craig Latta's Caffeine took further. A universally respected programmer in the Squeak and Pharo communities, widely loved for her generosity and joy.
