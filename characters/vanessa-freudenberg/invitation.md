# In memoriam — Vanessa Freudenberg (d. 2025)

*Not an invitation — a tribute. Legacy K-line, honored with sourced material only.*
[Portrayal standards](../../schemas/portrayal-standards.yml)

Vanessa Freudenberg wrote **SqueakJS**, bringing a live Squeak Smalltalk virtual machine into
the web browser — the foundation that Craig Latta's **Caffeine** builds on, and a cornerstone of
the live-object, live-in-the-browser lineage this repo follows. She was a beloved, brilliant
presence in the Squeak and Pharo communities.

We celebrate her documented public work; we do not speak as her.

— Don Hopkins, Micropolis Class
