# A Repo Show with Henry Minsky 🧠

> Society of Mind, *running* — many small agents making a mind, and a manufacturing line legible.

**Henry Minsky** is co-founder and CTO of **Leela AI**, whose visual intelligence reads activity in
video — principles that trace straight back to the MIT AI Lab his father co-founded. Earlier he was
a software architect at **Laszlo Systems** (the **OpenLaszlo** declarative app framework). He grew up
*inside* the AI Lab with his twin Juliana and sister [Margaret](../../characters/margaret-minsky/README.md),
playing in the children's computer lab while grad students watched children learn.
