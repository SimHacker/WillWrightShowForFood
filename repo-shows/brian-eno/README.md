# A Repo Show with Brian Eno 🎛️ *(dream guest)*

> Playing with Time, again — generative systems across media, twenty years after the Long Now talk.

**Brian Eno** — pioneer of **ambient and generative music** (*Music for Airports*, *Bloom*,
*77 Million Paintings*), producer, visual artist, and the person who **named "The Long Now."** This is
a **dream seed**: a show we'd love to make, contingent entirely on Brian's interest, reached **gently
via [Will Wright](../../characters/will-wright/) and [Scott Draves](../../characters/scott-draves/)**
(who hosted Brian and Don in 2006).

## The thread

Generative systems as one idea in different clothes — **music** (Eno), **visual art** (Scott's
vote-and-genetics [Electric Sheep](../../characters/scott-draves/)), and **simulation**
(Will's SimCity/Spore). *Rules, time, surrender — and what AI actually changes* now that image and
music generation have caught up to where the three of them were each pointing.

## What we'd get into

- **Generative across media** — the same engine, three art forms.
- **Oblique Strategies as prompts** — draw a card, watch it bend a live pipeline.
- **Scenius, made literal** — Eno's collective-genius idea ↔ Electric Sheep.
- **The 77 Million Paintings move, built small** — layered, never-repeating composition.
- **A gestural, musical pie menu** (Don's pitch) — direction = timbre, the curve between = the sound.
- **🐅 Taking Tiger Mountain, by transformation** — Doug Hilsinger & Caroleen Beatty's all-by-hand
  cover that Eno praised; the **stretch goal** is to track down [Doug](../../characters/doug-hilsinger/)
  and invite him to the reunion.
- **The Long Now lens** — what's worth *seeding* now for a 10,000-year audience?
