## How this fits the larger project

Will's Spore GDC keynote marks the industry turn toward **procedural content** — player-generated creatures and universes instead of hand-authored expansion packs alone. This repo holds **both** lineages:

- **Procedural / scalable** — Spore, MOOLLM, Micropolis Class homefun
- **Artisanal / familial** — SimFreaks, SimProv Wedding Playset, Transmogrifier, the grandmother-with-Photoshop story

Neither cancels the other. The [intro narrative](../../INTRO-NARRATIVE.md) explains how Micropolis publishing hosts the hand-crafted magnum opus while the engine learns from Will's procedural pivot.

Will Wright's **Game Developers Conference** keynote demo (March 11, 2005) introduced **Spore**'s procedural content pipeline — creature editor, sharing, and the shift from hand-authored expansion packs to player-generated universes.
