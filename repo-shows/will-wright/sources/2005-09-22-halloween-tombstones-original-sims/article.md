# Make Personalized Halloween Tombstones for The Sims

You can make your own free personalized Halloween tombstone for The Sims! Engrave your own tombstones, download them, and email them to your friends. Visit the [Original Sims cemetery](http://www.originalsims.com/cemetery) and pay respects to more than 1200 people's and Sims' memories.

You have permission to download and play with these Sims objects for free, and give copies to your family, friends and neighbors. You are even free to use these objects as bases to make your own custom Sims objects! Download the free Sims object creation tool, **The Sims Transmogrifier**, to clone Sims objects and modify their appearance.

Original Sims was a collaboration of **SimSlice**, **SimFreaks**, **SimBabes**, and Don Hopkins — same family as the Dumbold Voting Machine and the content-catalog work.
