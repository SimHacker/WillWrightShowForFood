> I'd like to see the game running on the machine when it is at a final state, if not sooner. It makes sense to me to perhaps get a couple of our QA guys to hammer on it a bit and see if they turn up anything.

## Don's VMware QA playbook

For EA staff without Linux laptops:

1. **VMware Server** (free registration): http://www.vmware.com/products/free_virtualization.html  
2. **OLPC OS images** for emulation: http://wiki.laptop.org/go/OS_images_for_emulation  
3. Alternative: stock **Fedora Core** VM (full dev environment)  
4. **16-bit color depth** required — config edits; Don offered walkthrough  
5. Install: unpack `SimCity_fedora.tgz` from Don's private OLPC test area  

Build state: **stable for testing**, playable, **no sound**. Don paused further dev until relicensing is certain.

Don offered: loan OLPC hardware, on-site EA visit (Redwood Shores or Will's Berkeley place), GDC demo with **SJ Klein** — multiplayer over OLPC wireless mesh.
