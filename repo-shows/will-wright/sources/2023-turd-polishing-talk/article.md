## The Sims story (1999–2000)

Don told his manager the code was in bad shape and needed cleanup before ship. The reply:

> **"Don, your job is TURD POLISHING. If you can just make your turd nice and shiny, we will ship it, and everybody will be happy with you, because that is what we hired you to do."**

Don got **a few weeks** to overhaul the worst parts anyway — "be careful what you ask for, or you might have to be the one who shovels out all the shit."

Useful oral-history frame for [2022-shamedic-maxis-history-qa](../2022-shamedic-maxis-history-qa/) (pre-ship crunch questions).
