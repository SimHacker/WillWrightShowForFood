# Transmogrifier — story and sources

## Why "Transmogrifier?"

> Because it's a real old word (since ~1650), and its letters rearrange to spell **"I Fart Iron Germs"**.

Will Wright checked trademark concerns (May 2000) and called off the lawyers after finding prior shareware uses of the name.

## What it is

A simple Sims 1 content tool: clone an object, edit catalog text/price, export/import **BMP sprites** for paint-program editing. Like SimShow — limited scope, easy to use.

## What it is not

- Not supported by Maxis/EA
- Not a behavior editor (that's **Edith** / SimAntics)
- Not 3D — 2D bitmaps only

Future tools were planned to extend the XML format to slots, sounds, walls, relationships, etc.
