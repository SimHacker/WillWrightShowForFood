# Maxis & The Sims — people

Verified public roster of people important to **The Sims** and Maxis history. Spelling checked against MobyGames, interviews, Wikipedia, Polygon, PCGamesN, and primary sources.

This is **not** the complete internal personnel list (that stays private). It's generous about who counts as important — founders, core team, voices, leadership, and key influences.
