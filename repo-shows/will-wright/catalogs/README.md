# Content catalogs

**Six doors, one house.** Four decades of artisanal Sims custom content — plus Maxis metadata and the platform to keep making it.

## SimFreaks — [`simfreaks/`](simfreaks/)

**Heather Alvey** — ~26 years of object suites, deep SimAntics, themed collections. One of the
deepest artisanal catalogs in Sims 1 history. Storefront meets OpenLaszlo CMS essay in
[`sources/`](../sources/2005-09-18-simfreaks-content-catalog-laszlo/).

---

## SimSlice — [`simslice/`](simslice/)

**Steve Alvey** — *SliceCity*: SimCity nested inside The Sims as playable content. Partner brand
to SimFreaks; the Alveys share front-row seats on the show.

---

## SimProv — [`simprov/`](simprov/)

**Wedding playset** — Crowd Sitter, Cupid, Hope Chest, Donna's craft layer. Inclusion agitprop
built when marriage equality was still contested; opposite pole from the Dumbold Voting Machine.
See [`procedural-rhetoric-inclusion-agitprop.md`](../assets/sims-series/procedural-rhetoric-inclusion-agitprop.md).

---

## Zombie Sims — [`zombie-sims/`](zombie-sims/)

Heather + Steve's current magnum-opus expansion — the crew they bring to the front row
(`attending_with: simfreaks-simslice` in audience seating).

---

## Maxis — [`maxis/`](maxis/)

Metadata catalog — killer downloads, object pointers, Steam / Internet Archive / web links.
Archivist layer; not a replacement for EA's official stores.

---

## Micropolis Home — [`micropolis-home/`](micropolis-home/)

**Create · publish · share** — federation platform underneath the legacy brands. Web-native
Transmogrifier, RugOMatic, body editors, CARD dirs, Exchange revival. Where new releases and git
artifacts live while SimFreaks/SimSlice keep their storefronts.

---

## Micropolis Home vs the legacy catalogs

The legacy brands are **catalogs**: curated bodies of work with their own fan bases.

**Micropolis Home** is the **platform**:

1. **Create** — web-native authoring + MOOLLM-assisted tools
2. **Publish** — federation CARD dirs, hosted content repos, round-trippable saves
3. **Share** — Exchange revival, family album stories, composable playsets

SimFreaks, SimSlice, SimProv, and Zombie Sims federate **into** Micropolis Home.
