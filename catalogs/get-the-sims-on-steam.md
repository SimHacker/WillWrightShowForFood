# Get The Sims on Steam

**Buy the game.** We mean it.

The custom content, catalogs, and tools in this repo assume you have a **legal copy of The Sims 1**
(and expansions you care about). The best path today is **[The Sims on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3314070/The_Sims/)**
— EA's current re-release. Get it there before you install SimFreaks, SimSlice, SimProv, or
Transmogrifier-era fan content.

## Why we say this out loud

Don Hopkins and many contributors **made** this game — UI, tools, objects, inclusivity work, the
Exchange culture that became SimFreaks and Micropolis Home. For twenty-six years fans have done
what Will Wright described in 1996: treat games as **train sets**, swap data, extend microworlds.
That is the hobby model. It is not piracy cosplay.

We want EA to see this project as:

- **Revenue** — Steam Sims 1 sales driven by nostalgia, family play, and a living modding scene
- **Publicity** — documented history, reunion shows, archivist respect, not another cease-and-desist story
- **Continuity** — the same fan energy that kept The Sims alive now has a public, citable home

We are not asking permission to exist. We are **recommending the product** and documenting the
lineage honestly. Trademark respect applies; so does giving credit where money should flow.

## What this repo is not

- Not a warez mirror — see [`maxis/CATALOG.yml`](maxis/CATALOG.yml) metadata-only policy
- Not a replacement for EA's store — links out to Steam, Internet Archive, and official channels
- Not hostile to rights holders — [`../process/licensing-micropolis-enterprise.yml`](../process/licensing-micropolis-enterprise.yml)
